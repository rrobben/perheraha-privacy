Privacy Policy — Perheraha

Perheraha is a personal family finance tracking app for Android. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how your data is handled.

Data Storage

All data you enter into Perheraha — including transactions, account balances, family member names, loans, and subscriptions — is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to any server or third party.

Data Collection

  • We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking
  • We do not require an internet connection
  • We do not access any data outside of the app

App Lock & Authentication

Perheraha includes an optional App Lock feature that restricts access to the app using a PIN and/or biometric authentication (fingerprint, face unlock).

  • Your PIN is stored encrypted on your device using the Android Keystore system — it is never transmitted anywhere
  • The app never accesses or stores raw biometric data; biometric authentication is handled entirely by the Android operating system and never leaves your device
  • Disabling App Lock in Settings permanently removes the stored PIN from your device

Data Deletion

  • You can delete all your data at any time from the app's Settings ("Clear all data & restart")
  • Uninstalling the app also deletes all data permanently

Permissions

Perheraha may request the following device permissions:

  • File system access — solely for importing CSV bank statement files; no files are uploaded or shared
  • Biometric hardware access — only used for the optional App Lock feature when enabled by you

Data Export

Perheraha includes a backup feature that lets you export your data as a JSON file. This export is entirely user-initiated — the app will never share your data automatically. Exported files are saved to your device or shared via your device's share sheet at your discretion. No data is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

Perheraha is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.
